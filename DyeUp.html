1.0 Background

This smart beer-dye device detects when a ball hits the line and lights up an LED for 10 seconds, indicating a fowl.

During Fall semester 2017 I tackled my most challenging yet rewarding project: Designing, 3D printing, programming, and assembling a prosthetic arm for under $120. The prosthetic was commissioned by a real client and was customized as such.
