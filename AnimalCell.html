During undergrad, I worked as a designer for the non-profit, "Feelin' DNA" at UNC. "Feelin' DNA" is predicated on the idea that blind students deserve equal access to a solid STEM education. With this non-profit, I 3D model and print scientific models for blind elementary school-aged students. Examples are listed below.
With every project, I try to vary the textures of each model so they are distinguishable simply through touch. These models were designed in SolidWorks and printed on an Ultimaker.
