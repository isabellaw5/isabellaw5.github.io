"Feelin' DNA" 3D Design Portfolio

I work as a designer for the non-profit, "Feelin' DNA" at UNC. "Feelin' DNA" is predicated on the idea that blind students deserve equal access to a solid STEM education. With this non-profit, I 3D model and print scientific models for blind elementary school-aged students. Examples are listed below.
With every project, I try to vary the textures of each model so they are distinguishable simply through touch. These models were designed in SolidWorks, and I am trained and certified by UNC Chapel-Hill to use a 3-D printer.
